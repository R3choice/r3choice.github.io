002 : Lost in Space

After crash-landing on an alien planet, the Robinson family fight against all odds to survive and escape, but they're surrounded by hidden dangers.

"This show is epic and great for more than the sci-fi lovers!"

Review:

The acting is superb! The CGI is more than believable. The script has you on the edge of your seat with every episode! I will agree, the science is stretched ...as with MOST movies (sorry all you LIS haters, but other action movies you gave high ratings to defy science just as much, if not more....e.g. cars don't blow up when shot with bullets....), but as with all movies, LIS is ENTERTAINMENT, not a science class. We go to school to learn correct science. Good job everyone who helped bring LIS back to the screen!!!!! Excellent stories! Excellent cinematography! And two thumbs up on all the budget peeps and how you pulled it all off! There's great adventures, well rounded humor for all ages, heart felt love, villains that make you angry and yell at your screen, and scenes that leave you awake at night pondering what will happen next! We connected with every actor and we were bummed when we finished the last episode. We can't wait for the next season! Bring it back Netflix! Please!
