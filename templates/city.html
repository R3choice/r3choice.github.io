004 : Invisible City

An underground world is inhabited by mythical creatures evolved from a deep lineage of Brazilian folklore. One detective who finds himself caught in a murder investigation that puts him in the middle of a battle between these two worlds.

"Originality, mistery and fantasy perfectly mixed!"

Review:

I'm so impressed with this series! The unique storyline got my attention right away, since it explores brazilian folklore in a way never done before. But the quality of script, acting, photography and effects overcame all my expectations. The oppening is beautifully made, like everything else in the serie. It's a must watch if you love mistery and fantasy, specially with such a unique plot! Orgulho por essa produção! Que venham mais!
